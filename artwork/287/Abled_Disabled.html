Artwork Story

Exhibited Laguna Art Museum 1998-9 "Life Lessons: How Art Can Change Your Life"

Judith Spence

In depicting the amazing feats of the disabled man, Stein calls into question the whole notion of disability. But this also congruent with Inconsolable. What is devastatingly injured is not miraculously made whole; something else has to happen from another part of the person.
