Artwork Story

Exhibited Laguna Art Museum 1998-9 "Life Lessons: How Art Can Change Your Life"

Judith Spence

Carrasco uses Frida Kahlo as model and as metaphor to break free of the constraints of cultural expectation, as a Latina woman (cutting the braid) and as a contemporary artist (Bic is the backbone).
