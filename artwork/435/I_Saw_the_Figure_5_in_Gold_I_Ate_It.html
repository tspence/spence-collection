I Saw the Figure 5 in Gold & I Ate It

Created 1988 Acquired 1989124

"Demuth work as slice of bread, with bite out"

Artwork Story

Donated to Laguna Art Museum in 1999 as one of 109 works.
Exhibited Laguna Art Museum 1998-9 "Life Lessons: How Art Can Change Your Life"

Stuart Spence

Judy and I both liked Demuth's "Figure 5", and when we saw Dave Rushing's sketch for this, we said :"We love it - we'll buy it when it's finished". Later we knew it was a perilous offer. He earned our respect by not reworking it into what he thought we might prefer, and for not taking advantage of the unspecified price. And we still love it.
