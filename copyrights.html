What are the copyrights on the images of the artworks?

The Spence Collection is a website that contains images of artworks collected by Judy and Stuart Spence. Some of these artworks are still owned by Judy and Stuart Spence, while others have been donated to various museums. The images on this website are provided courtesy of the artists, who retain copyright ownership over the artwork and its likeness. For information about licensing or reusing the images contained on this website, please contact the artists directly as the Spence family does not control reproduction rights for these artworks.
